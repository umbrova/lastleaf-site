How it works

01

Install and forget

Add Lastleaf to Chrome. It runs silently in the background — no setup, no accounts, no configuration needed.

02

Close tabs freely

Lastleaf captures every tab you close (above a minimum time threshold you control) and groups them by topic automatically.

03

Explore the graph

Open the dashboard to see your tab history as an interactive graph — clusters of related tabs, time spent, and one-click restore.

Features

🗂️

Topic clusters

Closed tabs are automatically grouped by topic using local NLP — no cloud processing, no data leaving your device.

🕸️

Interactive graph

See your browsing sessions as a visual map. Clusters float, connect, and reveal how your work actually flows.

⏱️

Time tracking

Each cluster shows how long you spent browsing that topic — useful for understanding where your attention really went.

🔒

Private by default

All tab data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is synced, sold, or shared. Uninstall to delete everything.

🚫

Excluded domains

Add domains you never want captured — banking sites, email, or anything sensitive. Lastleaf will skip them entirely.

📦

90-day history

Full tab records kept for 90 days (adjustable), then automatically compressed to lightweight summaries to save space.
